Universal Doctrine

Inspired by the timeless teachings of Guru Nanak

Core Principles

  • One Universal Creator — beyond labels, religions, and boundaries
  • Truthful living over ritualistic practices
  • Selfless service (Seva) and compassion for all beings
  • Equality of all humans — regardless of gender, caste, or creed
  • Inner discipline through Naam (spiritual connection)
  • Honest work and sharing with those in need
  • Living fearlessly and with humility

Vision

This doctrine aims to serve as a universal path for humanity — grounded in love, peace, and harmony — beyond the divisions of religion or power. It welcomes every soul seeking truth.

Spread the Light
